This homework only practices Class 7 content: healthy habits and the present perfect.If + past simple, would/could + base verb).

1. Reading

Healthy Habits: Tom's Story

Tom used to have a very unhealthy lifestyle. He would skip breakfast every morning, eat fast food for lunch, and spend his evenings on the couch. The turning point came when he realized he was getting tired just walking up the stairs to his office.

Since then, Tom has made significant changes. He has joined a gym and now does a mix of cardio and strength training three times a week. He has also transformed his eating habits — he now has breakfast with fresh fruit and whole grains every morning, and he prepares healthy lunches for the whole work week every Sunday.

The results have been impressive. Tom has noticed a big improvement in his energy levels. He sleeps better at night and no longer gets that afternoon tiredness. Exercise has helped him manage stress, and his concentration at work has improved too. His advice: start with small changes and build gradually.
